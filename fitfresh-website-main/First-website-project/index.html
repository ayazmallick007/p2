Our method

It's as easy as 1, 2, 3

1

Provide us with your bodyweight and fitness goals.

Our AI will provide you a personalized meal plan according to your fitness goals, consisting of the proper macros (protein, fats, and carbs), so you can achieve your goals easily with no extra hassle

2

Once provided your weekly meal plan, approve it.

You'll be able to change any of the ingredients to suit your specific caloric intake and macro requirements based on our suggestions and research.

3

Receive or schedule your meals.

The closest chef to you will begin preparing your weekly meals and we will aim to deliver it to you whenever you'd like. You can also change your delivery schedule and address.
